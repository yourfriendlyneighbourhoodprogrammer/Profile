Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic.
  3. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil.
